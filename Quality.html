IN-HOUSE QUALITY CHECK EQUIPMENT
     The Quality Assurance Laboratory of Aluka is equipped with a modern testing facility to ensure 
    the highest product standards.

    Optical Emission Colour Spectrometer  for controlling alloy chemical composition
    Hardness and UTS machine  for mechanical testing of profiles
    Electrical Conductivity meter  for testing of Electrical conductivity of profiles in % IACS
    Metallurgical Microscope  for determining microstructure of grains and Inclusion in casting
    as well as profiles
    Profile projector  for determining minute details of critical dimensions
    Apart from the given equipment, our trained workforce from the Quality Control Department 
    a constant watch over the processes being carried out in the plant. Research and Development 
    under the guidance of technical experts is an ongoing process for continuous improvement of
    the product.
